01 — Who I am

A builder who never stops learning.

If I had to describe my style in one line: curious, hands-on, and allergic to standing still. I’ve spent 15 years widening my lane rather than guarding it — Android, distributed backends, cloud security, GenAI platforms, and now my own company. Principal Engineer at Microsoft (GenAI, multi-agent, RAG), SDE at Amazon (Alexa, Prime, Transportation), ingestion at Palo Alto Networks.

The constant isn’t a technology — it’s the loop: get curious, learn fast, build the thing, ship it, learn again. It’s why I can turn a weekend into a playable AR game, a stray idea into a trading system, or a new field into a real product. I’m an eternal learner by temperament, and right now the thing I’m learning hardest is the one most engineers avoid: how to sell and distribute what I build.
